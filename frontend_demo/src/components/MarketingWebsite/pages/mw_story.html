COVID-19 AND THE STATE OF KIOSK USE

Background

With the advent of the coronavirus pandemic of 2020, and with it’s threat continuing to worry consumers and businesses alike, the world of marketing and customer service has seen a dramatic shift. A change of focus for businesses, placing priority on the safety of their customers.

Notably, the use of interactive kiosks, namely in popular shopping centres and stores, once a useful and convenient utility for navigation and advertisement, have been understandably restricted to ensure the safety of shoppers. These kiosks are touch operated, posing the thread of spreading disease.

With these touch-operated kiosks out of order and clearly obsolete given recent developments, a modernized, safer solution is required.

STUDENTS TURNED DEVELOPERS

The team

In 2021, a group of students based at the University of Wollongong were tasked to solve this problem, and as students turned developers, they are agile and adaptive in their approach. Well-knit, driven, and keen to breathe new life into a technology made obsolete, the students iterated upon hundreds of changes and additions to build a software like a castle, brick by brick.

SAFE, RELIABLE AND CUSTOMISABLE

Our objectives and solution

With primary concerns for customer safety, namely preventing the spread of disease through interacting with the kiosks in question, we designed a reliable and convenient touch-free alternative using QR technology to keep kiosk users safe.

Simply put: QR-operated kiosks.

Additionally, in the interests of businesses seeking independence and a hassle-free experience, the service had to be customisable and intuitive, but also reliable. We wanted the business-end users of QRMAX to be self-reliant and confident when implementing our software and without any tedious or boring training seminars, new users hosting QRMAX kiosks can create a personalised and professional interface design unique to their business. We wanted our software to feel natural and easy to navigate. With a plethora of handy tools and a slick interface, we believe this has been achieved.

THE FRUITS OF OUR LABOUR

The product

QRMAX had humble beginnings as a small and untitled project, but rapidly developed into a thorough and streamlined solution. Featuring a touch-free, QR-operated alternative to user interaction, in addition to accounts management and kiosk interface customisation, QRMAX is a powerful proof for the future of practical and safe kiosk design.

QRMAX is a web-based JavaScript application built upon the modern and critically-acclaimed MERN stack development suite, meaning it won’t be relying on any dodgy or outdated, thus unsafe platforms to get the job done. As a web-app, there is no complex installation process and no expensive hardware requirements or hurdles. All your system needs is a modern internet browser and a solid internet connection. Within no time you could have a safe and reliable kiosk up and running for your customers.

What’s to lose? It’s safe, convenient, and completely free. Be a part of the future of safe kiosk design and get started.
